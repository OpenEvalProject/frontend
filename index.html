OpenEvalProject

The OpenEvalProject provides transparent, systematic evaluation of large language model (LLM) performance in scientific peer review. We compare LLM-generated reviews against traditional peer reviews to assess their accuracy, consistency, and reliability in identifying claims and evaluating scientific evidence.
